Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers.

Delivery & Tracking

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within the UK takes 2-4 working days. We also offer next-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM Monday–Thursday.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email. You can also view your order status by logging into your My Account dashboard.

Returns & Refunds

What is your returns policy?

We accept returns on unopened and unused items within 30 days of delivery. For hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns on opened wigs, hair extensions, or skincare products. Read our full Returns Policy here.

How long do refunds take?

Once we receive your returned item, your refund will be processed to your original payment method (via Stripe) within 5-10 business days.

Our Products

Are your products authentic?

Yes, 100%. We source all of our products directly from the brands or their official UK distributors to ensure premium quality and authenticity for our customers.

I don't know what my hair type is. Can you help?

Absolutely! Feel free to drop us an email via our Contact Page with a description or photo of your hair, and our team will recommend the best products for your crown.
