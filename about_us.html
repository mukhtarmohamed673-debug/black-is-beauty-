About Beauty In Black

Celebrating melanin-rich beauty, one product at a time.

Our Story

Born from passion, built for you

Beauty In Black was founded in Ealing, London, with one mission: to create a space where Black women, men, and children could find premium hair care, skincare, and beauty products made for and celebrating melanin-rich skin and hair textures.

We believe that beauty is not one-size-fits-all. That's why every product in our store is hand-picked, tested, and curated with our community in mind.
