Returns Policy

Last updated: April 2026

30-Day Returns

Return most items within 30 days of delivery.

Unopened Items Only

Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Full Refund

Refunds processed within 5–10 business days.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date
  • Unopened, unused, and in their original packaging
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned once opened:

  • Hair extensions, wigs, and hairpieces
  • Skincare products (creams, serums, oils)
  • Makeup and cosmetics
  • Any item marked as "Final Sale"

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact us at our contact page with your order number and reason for return.
  2. We'll send you a Return Authorisation (RA) number within 2 business days.
  3. Pack your item securely and include your RA number on the package.
  4. Ship the item to the return address provided. Return postage is at the customer's expense unless the item is faulty.

Faulty or Incorrect Items

If you received a faulty, damaged, or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We will arrange a free return and send a replacement or issue a full refund at no cost to you.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you by email. If approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Questions? Get in touch with our team — we're happy to help.
