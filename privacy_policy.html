Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit Black Is Beauty, we collect information necessary to process your orders and improve your experience. This includes:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe. We do not store your full card details on our servers.
  • Technical Data: IP address and browser type collected via cookies to optimize site performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data allows us to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders for our premium hair and beauty products.
  • Send order tracking updates and customer support responses.
  • Improve our website layout and product selection based on user behavior.
  • Send marketing emails (only if you have explicitly opted-in via our newsletter form).

3. Third-Party Services

We never sell your data. However, we share necessary information with trusted partners to run our store:

  • Stripe: To securely process your payments.
  • Courier Services: To deliver your parcels to your doorstep.
  • Google Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site.

4. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact our data privacy officer via the form below.
